Operator Precedence
-------------------
- The ! (NOT) operator has the highest precedence than && (AND) and || (OR)
- The && (AND) operator has higher precedence than || (OR)
- Precedence can be altered using a pair of ( )
- NOTE: When in doubt of precedence, use a pair ( ) to group operators
