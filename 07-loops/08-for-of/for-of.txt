For...Of
--------
- It is a nice & easy way of iterating over arrays & other iterable objects
    - Includes strings, maps, sets, nodelists, typedarrays, DOM collections, argument objexts, generators
    - As well as user-defined iterables

- NOTE: It is NOT SUPPORTED on Internet Explorer browser

- Use case:
    - There is NO need for the index value of an iterable
    - Just need to access the individual element of an iterable

- Reference (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Statements/for...of)


Syntax
------
for (variable of iterable)
  statement
