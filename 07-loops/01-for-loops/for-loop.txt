For Loop
---------
- The for statement creates a loop that consists of:
    - 3 optional expressions enclosed in parenthesis & separated by semi-colons
        - initialization
        - condition
        - afterthought (increment || decrement)
    - A block statement to be executed in the loop

- Reference (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Statements/for)


Syntax
------
for (initialization; condition; afterthought)
  statement
