Parsing Number Of Awards
------------------------
- Parsing the selected movie's "awards" data
    - Contains inconsistent set of data that revolves around
        - Oscar
        - Wins
        - Nominations

- To address the inconsistency of data
    - Find all different awards data
    - Convert them to numbers
    - Sum them up

    - The movie with the most (higher) number wins
        - Inaccurate but fits the purposes of this application
