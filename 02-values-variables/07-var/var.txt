var
---
- Declares a globally or function-scoped variables, optionally initializing to a value
- It WAS the old-way of making variables and now REPLACED by 'let'
- Reference (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Statements/var)

Syntax
------
var <variable_name>;
var <variable_name> = <value>;

Example
-------
var tripDistance = 7.4;
